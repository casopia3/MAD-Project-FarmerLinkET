 🌾 FarmerLink ET

 **Connecting Ethiopia's farmers to knowledge, tools, and markets.*

## 📖 Project Description

**FarmerLink ET** is an Android mobile utility application designed specifically for Ethiopian smallholder farmers. The app provides an all-in-one productivity platform that combines a smart crop calendar, real-time local market prices, weather alerts, and a pest/disease identification guide — all available in **Amharic** and with **offline support** for low-connectivity rural areas.

Ethiopian smallholder farmers — who represent over 80% of the country's agricultural workforce — face critical information gaps that reduce productivity and income. Farmers in regions such as Sidama, Oromia, and SNNPR lack timely access to accurate crop market prices, localized weather forecasts, and guidance on pest and disease management. FarmerLink ET addresses these gaps by centralizing essential farming tools in one easy-to-use mobile app, empowering farmers to make data-driven decisions — planting at the right time, selling at the right price, and protecting their crops before losses occur.

## ✨ Core MVP Features

| # | Feature | Description |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | 📅 **Crop Calendar & Planting Scheduler** | Personalized seasonal planting, fertilizing, and harvesting schedule with reminders |
| 2 | 💰 **Local Market Price Checker** | Up-to-date prices for major crops at nearby markets |
| 3 | ⛅ **Weather Alerts & Forecast** | Localized 7-day forecasts with farm-relevant alerts |
| 4 | 🐛 **Pest & Disease Diagnostic Guide** | Visual offline guide with Amharic descriptions and treatments |
| 5 | 📊 **Expense & Yield Tracker** | Log seasonal costs and harvest output to track profit/loss |
